x Squared is Congruent to 1 iff x is Congruent to Plus or Minus One Mod p
\[ x ^ 2 \equiv 1 \pmod{ p } \iff x \equiv \pm 1 \pmod{ p } \]

\( \implies \) Suppose that \( x ^ 2 \equiv 1 \pmod{ p } \) therefore \( p \mid x ^ 2 - 1 = \left( x + 1 \right) \left( x - 1 \right) \) therefore \( p \mid \left( x + 1 \right) \) or \( p \mid \left( x - 1 \right) \) so that \( x \equiv \pm 1 \pmod{ p } \)

\( \impliedby \) Suppose that \( x \equiv \pm 1 \pmod{ p } \) therefore \( x ^ 2 \equiv 1 \pmod{ p } \) as needed.
