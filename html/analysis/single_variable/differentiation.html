derivative
Let `f` be a function and let `a` be a point in its domain, then the derivative of f at a is defined as: `lim_(h->0)(f(a+h)-f(a))/(h)`, when it exists and is denoted by `f^'(a)` or `(d)/(dx)f(a)`
partial derivative
Let `U` be an open subset of `RR^n`, and let `f:U -> RR` be a function, then the partial derivative with respect to the `i`-th variable, `x_i`, of `f` at `a=(a_1,...,a_n) in U` is defined as: `lim_(h->0)(f(a_1,...,a_i + h,...,a_n)-f(a_1,...,a_i,...,a_n))/(h)` when it exists, and is denoted by `(del)/(del x_i) f(a)`
