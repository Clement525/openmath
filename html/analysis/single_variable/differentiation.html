derivative
Let `f` be a function and let `a` be a point in its domain, then the derivative of f at a is defined as: `lim_(h->0)(f(a+h)-f(a))/(h)`, when it exists and is denoted by `f^'(a)`
partial derivative
Let `f` be a function of `n>0` variables and let `a` be a point in the domain of one variable `x_0`, with the others remaining constant, then the partial derivative of f at a is defined as: `lim_(h->0)(f(a+h)-f(a))/(h)`, when it exists and is denoted by `(del f(a,x_1,x_2,...,x_n))/(del x_0)`
