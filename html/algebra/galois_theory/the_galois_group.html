Automorphism
If \( E \) is a field, then an automorphism of \( E \) is an isomorphism of \( E \) with itself.
Automorphism Fixed Pointwise
If \( E / F \) is a field extension, then an automorphism \( \sigma \) of \( E \) fixes \( F \) pointwise if \( \sigma \left( c \right) = c \) for every \( c \in F \)
Automorphism Fixed Pointwise Takes Roots to Roots
Let \( f \left( x \right) \in F \left[ X \right] \) and let \( E / F \) be an extension field of \( F \). If \( \sigma : E \to E \) is an automorphism fixing \( F \) pointwise , and if \( \alpha \in E \) is a root of \( f \left( x \right) \) then \( \sigma \left( \alpha \right) \) is also a root of \( f \left( x \right) \)
Suppose that \( f \left( x \right) = a_0 + a_1 x^1 + \cdots a_n x^n \) with \( a _ i \in F \) and therefore \( a_0 + a_1 \alpha^1 + \cdots a_n \alpha^n = 0 \), now since \( \sigma \) is a homomorphism, we have that \[ 0 = \sigma \left( 0 \right) = \sigma \left( a_0 + a_1 \alpha^1 + \cdots a_n \right) = \sigma \left( a _ 0 \right) + \sigma \left( a _ 1 \right) \sigma \left( \alpha \right) + \cdots + \sigma \left( a _ n \right) \sigma \left( \alpha \right) ^ n \] And since \( \sigma \) fixes \( F \) pointwise we know that this is equal to \[ a _ 0 + a _ 1 \sigma \left( \alpha \right) + \cdots + a _ n \sigma \left( \alpha \right) _ n = 0 \] which means that \( \sigma \left( \alpha \right) \) is a root of \( f \left( x \right) \) as needed.
