Dihedral Group
A dihedral group \( D _ n \) is a group of order \( 2n \) containing an element \( a \) of order \( n \) and an element \( b \) of order \( 2 \) such that \( bab = a ^ { -1 } \)
Elements of a Dihedral Group
Suppose that \( D _ n \) is a dihedral group, then it's elements are given by \[ \left\{ e, a ^ 1, a ^ 2, \ldots , a ^ { n - 1 }, b e , b a ^ 1, b a ^ 2, \ldots ,b a ^ { n - 1 } \right\} \]
